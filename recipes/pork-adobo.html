Pork Adobo

Filipino Pork Adobo

Pork adobo is one of the main beloved dish in the Philippines. This braised pork belly cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and some spices, is great for everyday meal or family get-together that surely everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs Pork Belly
  • 1 tbsp Garlic
  • 1/2 White onion
  • 1 cup White vinegar
  • 1 cup Soy sauce
  • 3 Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp Black peppercorn

Steps

  1. Fry pork belly until golden and set aside
  2. Cook onion and garlic on the same pan and oil
  3. Combine cooked pork belly, garlic and onion
  4. Add black peppercorn, soy sauce, and bay leaves and stir
  5. Add white vinegar and don't stir
  6. Boil ingredients together. Stir occasionally.
